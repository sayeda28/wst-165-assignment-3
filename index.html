Hoover Dam

The Hoover Dam, also known as the Boulder Dam. Which holds back the mighty Colorado River forming Lake Mead, located on the Rizona-Nevada border on highway 93. A great place to visit with friends and family while visiting Las Vegas.

Seven Magic Mountain

Another attraction close to vegas is Seven Magic Mountains, only 25 minutes away from las vegas strip.Perfect for photoshoot, photos comes stunning and it's a great way to experience the magic of Mojave desert.
